Contact Bharat Modules, India's closed-network B2B industrial procurement platform, through our central operations team. We provide dedicated communication channels for enterprise buyers, defense contractors, Indian OEMs, and manufacturers who wish to source or list verified indigenous components. Our procurement desk handles RFQ submissions and bulk purchasing inquiries for IDDM-compliant components. All enterprise procurement requests are reviewed within a guaranteed 24-hour SLA response window.

For vendor onboarding and manufacturer compliance, Indian OEMs and factories can reach our compliance and audit team to initiate the Bharat Verified certification process. Certified manufacturers gain access to list their verified inventory on our closed network and receive direct RFQs from enterprise buyers across India. Platform and technical support is available for existing registered users experiencing API integration issues, account access problems, or institutional partnership inquiries.

Our registered headquarters is located in Basavanagudi, Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. Visits to our office require a prior appointment. You can also reach us by phone or WhatsApp at our direct line, available Monday through Friday between 0900 and 1800 IST. For procurement assistance, vendor verification status updates, technical support, or institutional and strategic partnership discussions, submit your enquiry using our contact form and our team will respond within one business day. Bharat Modules is committed to supporting the Make in India initiative through transparent, compliant, and traceable industrial supply chains.
