2. Non-Circumvention & Redirect Policy

To protect the integrity of the platform, the Parties agree to the following:

  • Disclosure: Customer names are revealed to the OEM only after the order is placed.
  • Prior Relationship Rule:
    A. If an OEM has traded with a Bharat Modules' introduced customer more than six (6) months prior to joining the platform, they must redirect all further trades with that customer through the platform.
    B. If an OEM has traded with a Bharat Modules' introduced customer in the six (6) months prior to joining the platform, they can continue to trade with those customers.
  • Verification: OEM must verify prior relationships by sharing relevant email history with Bharat Modules.
  • Bypass Penalty: If the OEM trades directly with a customer introduced via Bharat Modules, the platform reserves the right to immediately terminate the OEM's access and hold all pending payments as liquidated damages.
  • Post-Exit: The OEM is free to trade with any party without restriction only after formally exiting the platform.

The Bharat Modules manufacturer onboarding process is a secure four-step verification journey designed for Indian OEMs and industrial component manufacturers. To become a Bharat Verified manufacturer and list your products on India's closed B2B procurement network, you must complete all four phases: profile registration, document upload, terms and conditions acknowledgement, and mutual non-disclosure agreement signing.

In Step 1, you verify your registration profile by confirming your business name, email address, and phone number via OTP. You also select the component categories that best represent your manufacturing output, including propulsion systems, power and energy components, avionics and navigation hardware, command and communication devices, airframes and structures, and payloads and actuators. In Step 2, you upload the required compliance documents including your GST registration certificate, MSME certificate, company bank statement, factory or manufacturing license, and company PAN card. All documents must be in PDF format and under 2 MB.

Step 3 requires you to read and acknowledge all eight sections of the Bharat Modules Platform Terms and Conditions covering blind shipping protocols, communication rules, product quality requirements, documentation compliance, payment and escrow processes, milestone payment terms, order cancellation policies, and returns and logistics liability. Step 4 covers the Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (M-NDA), which governs the confidential exchange of business information between Bharat Modules and your organisation. Upon signing, your onboarding application is submitted for review. Our internal audit team verifies all documents within two business days, after which you receive full access to the Manufacturer Dashboard.
